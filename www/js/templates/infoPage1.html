Getting Here
Derry-Londonderry is the main city in the North-West of Ireland located about 70 miles from Belfast and 3 hours drive from Dublin.
By Air

There are regular flights directly into City of Derry Airport from London Stansted, Glasgow, Birmingham and Liverpool. Taxis are available from outside the arrivals area and typically cost around £12 into the city centre.

Belfast International Airport (1hr 15mins) and George Best Belfast City Airport (1hr 45mins) have dozens of flights daily to/from the UK and continental Europe as well as daily flights to/from Newark International. Dublin International Airport is around 3 hours from Derry. To get to/from the Belfast airports, make sure to check out our Official Transport Partner - Airporter. By Bus / Rail
Buses run from Belfast to Derry-Londonderry roughly every 30mins from 6:30 AM - midnight. The journey takes about 1hr 45mins. The train journey takes longer but it’s a beautiful trip. Details for both bus and rail journeys within Northern Ireland are available at Translink. Bus transport from Dublin, Galway, Sligo and other locations in the Republic of Ireland can be booked at Bus Éireann

Getting Around
Derry is a very walkable city and all the main festival venues are within just a few minutes of each other on foot. Taxis in Derry are convenient and inexpensive with fares to most destinations within the city under £5.
