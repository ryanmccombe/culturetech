The Company

Thereʼs little doubt that the worlds of art, culture and technology are coming together. We see this as a huge opportunity – for innovative arts and cultural organisations who want to stand out, for smart tech companies who want to ﬁgure it out and for cities and regions who want to tell their stories to the world.

At the very least, this convergence is an exciting conversation and CultureTECH Ltd is a company that wants to be at the centre of that conversation.

Based in Derry-Londonderry, N. Ireland – a tiny city on the edge of Europe – we produce an annual festival, work with a growing list of partners to deliver impactful innovation programmes and work with even more partners to perpetuate new ideas and conversations.

We are also in the process of planning several new events in the UK, Europe and N. America, are developing a suite of methodologies to connect the tech and arts/culture sectors and hope to create a Centre of Excellence in Cultural Technologies in Derry-Londonderry.

The company is steered by private-sector Board of Directors, alongside a number of industry advisors. We also work closely with several public sector partners including but not limited to the Department of Culture Arts and Leisure, Invest Northern Ireland, regeneration company ILEX, Derry City Council and the City of London Corporation.
